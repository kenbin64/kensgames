HOW TO PLAY
  • Pilot your fighter using arrow keys / WASD or on-screen touch controls.
  • Fire at enemy ships and avoid their projectiles. Your ship has a limited shield.
  • Survive each wave to advance. Enemies increase in speed and firepower with each wave.
  • Collect power-up drops from destroyed enemies � they appear briefly before fading.
  • In co-op mode, your team shares a combined wave counter. Coordinate fire to maximize survival.
  • Game ends when all players lose their shields. Your highest wave is recorded on the leaderboard.
