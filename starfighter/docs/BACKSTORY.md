# Starfighter — Backstory

## The Setting

**Year 2387. Earth orbit.**

Humanity has expanded across the solar system, but Earth remains home.
The United Earth Defense Force (UEDF) maintains orbital stations and a fleet of battleships to protect the homeworld.

---

## The Threat

An alien force — designation **Hive Sigma** — emerged from deep space six months ago.
Their ships are organic-metallic hybrids: grown, not built.
They don't communicate. They don't negotiate. They consume.

Their motherships drift through systems like predators, deploying swarms of fighters that strip planets of resources and life.

Earth is their next target.

---

## Your Ship

You fly a **UEDF Mk-IV Starfighter** — humanity's frontline interceptor.

- Single-seat cockpit with full instrument HUD
- Twin laser cannons for rapid fire
- Proton torpedo launcher with guided tracking
- Turbo thrust afterburner for pursuit and evasion
- Six degrees of freedom — full pitch, yaw, roll control

Your fighter launches from the **UEDF Battleship Resolute** — a capital-class carrier that serves as your mobile base.
It can repair, rearm, and refuel your ship between engagements.

---

## The Resolute

The Resolute is one of Earth's last heavy carriers.
Her hull has seen decades of service, patched and reinforced through countless engagements.

She carries a complement of fighters and maintains a defensive perimeter around Earth.
If the Resolute falls, the orbital defense line collapses.

**Protect her at all costs.**

---

## The Battle

Hive Sigma attacks in waves.
First, scout fighters probe the defenses.
Then, as the waves intensify, **motherships** appear — massive organic capital ships that can tear through a battleship's hull.

Each wave is harder than the last.
The aliens learn. They adapt. They don't stop.

---

## Your Mission

1. **Launch** from the Resolute's hangar bay
2. **Engage** and destroy all enemy fighters in each wave
3. **Prioritize** alien motherships — they attack the Resolute directly
4. **Return** to the Resolute between waves for repairs and rearming
5. **Survive** as long as possible

There is no final wave. There is no winning.
There is only **how long you hold the line**.

---

## The World Around You

- **Earth** looms below — blue, green, alive. Cloud patterns drift across its surface. The thin blue line of atmosphere glows at its edge. This is what you're fighting for.
- **The Sun** burns in the distance — a blazing star with a shimmering corona. Its light casts harsh shadows across every ship and station.
- **Orbital Station Kepler** — a human space station with an artificial gravity ring, visible in the distance. Civilian population. Another thing worth protecting.
- **The Stars** — thousands of them, scattered across the void. Ancient light from a universe that doesn't care about your war.

---

## Comm Chatter

During combat, you'll hear from:

- **Baseship (Resolute)**: Mission updates, warnings, status reports
- **Alpha Squadron**: Fellow pilots — callsigns Alpha-1 through Alpha-4
- **Warning System**: Threat alerts for capital ships and critical events

The voices keep you grounded. You're not alone out here.
Not yet.
