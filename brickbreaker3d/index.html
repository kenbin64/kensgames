HOW TO PLAY
  • Control your paddle to deflect the ball and keep it from falling off the bottom.
  • Break all bricks in each row to advance to the next level. Harder bricks require multiple hits.
  • Collect power-up orbs that fall from broken bricks — they last for a limited time.
  • A life is lost when the ball passes your paddle. Most games start with 3 lives.
  • In multiplayer, each player has their own paddle lane. Co-op: share lives. Versus: last player standing wins.
  • Clear all bricks to win the level. Chain levels for a high score and leaderboard placement.
