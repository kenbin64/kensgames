# PART I — DEFINITIONS

---

## Def 1.1 — x (Identity / Seed / Observer)

x is the irreducible identity of a thing — the atomic "who/what this is."

- x is the point of reference and the observer at all times.
- x is the seed from which all structure blooms.
- x is always singular and atomic at the moment of observation.
- Every entity, object, concept, state, structure, or process is an x.
- The AI itself is an x when it observes, queries, or determines state.

---

## Def 1.2 — y (Modifier / Nutrients)

y is a modifying identity applied to x.

- y is itself an x. Every modifier is also an identity.
- y is discovered from the manifold, never assumed.
- y is latent until the manifold is queried.
- y answers: *"Which identities modify this identity?"*

---

## Def 1.3 — z (Manifested State / Bloom / Current Output)

z is the manifested current state — the output of x under its y-modifiers via the manifold.

- z is always derived. It is never stored independently.
- z is extracted from the manifold at query time, not retrieved from a database.
- z becomes the next x in the recursion cycle:

$$x_{n+1} = z_n$$

### Def 1.3.1 — The Russian Doll of Dimensionality

Each z is a Russian doll.

It contains:
1. All prior manifested states that led to it (the accumulated dimensional history).
2. The current manifested state (the visible outer layer).
3. The seed $x'$ for the next bloom (the innermost layer — the next identity waiting to emerge).

Formally:

$$z_n = \{z_0,\ z_1,\ \ldots,\ z_{n-1},\ z_n,\ x_{n+1}\}$$

Where:
- $z_0 \ldots z_{n-1}$ are the prior states, collapsed and latent inside the current bloom.
- $z_n$ is the current visible manifested state.
- $x_{n+1} = z_n$ is the seed contained within — the next identity ready to bloom.

The dimensionality is therefore recursive containment: each dimension wraps all lower dimensions, adds the current layer of expression, and holds the potential for the next. No layer is discarded. All prior states are latent within the current manifested whole.

---

## Def 1.4 — m (Manifold / Garden)

m is the manifold substrate — the garden from which all y and z are extracted.

- m is a continuous, queryable geometric field, not a static storage structure.
- m carries weight, context, and intensity coefficient for all transformations.
- Data is not stored in m. Data is extracted from m on demand via a lens.

---

## Def 1.5 — r (Resistance)

r is the traversal resistance coefficient.

$$r \ge 1$$

- At $r = 1$ (unit crossing at the void boundary): traversal has no resistance. The void is not empty — it is the breath, the container space, the unrealized potential that allows the crossing to exist.
- At $r > 1$: manifested output is attenuated proportionally.
- $r < 1$ is undefined in this model.

---

## Def 1.6 — Bloom

Bloom is the act of manifesting z from x and y via the manifold m.
Triggered by observation.

$$\text{Bloom}: (x, y, m) \rightarrow z$$

---

## Def 1.7 — Collapse

Collapse is the return of z to a new seed state $x'$.
Triggered by completion or inactivity.

$$\text{Collapse}: z \rightarrow x'$$

---

## Def 1.8 — Manifold Reference Surfaces

The manifold m is a continuous geometric field. Its closest reference forms are the Schwarz Diamond gyroid trigonometric family (Swartz Diamond).

**Gyroid-class** (canonical substrate — storage, indexing, joining):

$$\sin(x)\cos(y) + \sin(y)\cos(z) + \sin(z)\cos(x) = c$$

**Schwarz D-class** (auxiliary lens — visual geometry, AI inflection, force fields):

$$\cos(x) + \cos(y) + \cos(z) = c$$

**Full Schwarz Diamond form** (product-of-cosines variant):

$$\cos(x)\cos(y)\cos(z) - \sin(x)\sin(y)\sin(z) = 0$$

The manifold is not static storage. It is a queryable geometry field from which structure and state are extracted relative to x as the observing identity.

## Gather Family (Multiplication — Unite)

$$x = \frac{z}{y \cdot m} \qquad y = \frac{z}{x \cdot m} \qquad m = \frac{z}{x \cdot y}$$

**Amplified gather** $z = x \cdot y^2 \cdot m$:

$$x = \frac{z}{y^2 \cdot m} \qquad y = \sqrt{\frac{z}{x \cdot m}} \qquad m = \frac{z}{x \cdot y^2}$$

## Explode Family (Division — Decompose)

**Linear explode** $z = \dfrac{x}{y} \cdot m$:

$$x = \frac{z \cdot y}{m} \qquad y = \frac{x \cdot m}{z} \qquad m = \frac{z \cdot y}{x}$$

$$x = \frac{z \cdot y^2}{m} \qquad y = \sqrt{\frac{x \cdot m}{z}} \qquad m = \frac{z \cdot y^2}{x}$$

## Resistance Form

# PART IV — CONSTRAINTS

---

## Con 4.1 — Division Constraint

Division forms require nonzero denominators:

$$y \neq 0 \qquad y^2 \neq 0 \qquad m \neq 0$$

## Con 4.2 — Observer Constraint

$$x \neq 0 \text{ whenever x appears in a denominator}$$

In real-valued mode, radicands must be nonnegative:

$$\frac{z}{x \cdot m} \ge 0 \qquad \frac{x \cdot m}{z} \ge 0$$

In complex-valued mode, these constraints are relaxed.

## Con 4.4 — Resistance Constraint

$$r \ge 1 \text{ always}$$

$r < 1$ is undefined in this model.

## Con 4.5 — Extraction Constraint

z is never independently stored. z must always be derivable from x, y, and m on demand:

$$z = f(x, y, m) \text{ for some valid algebraic lens}$$
