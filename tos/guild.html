PORTAL GENERAL TOS
GUILD TERMS OF SERVICE
Last updated: June 2025 · Applies in addition to the General TOS

1. GUILD MEMBERSHIP

By joining or creating a guild you agree to these Guild Terms in addition to the General Terms of Service. Guilds are player-run communities within the Portal. The Portal administrators are not responsible for day-to-day guild moderation.

2. CREATING A GUILD

  • Guild names must be 3–40 characters and must not impersonate other guilds or people.
  • Guild tags must be 2–5 alphanumeric characters (uppercase).
  • Guild names and tags may not contain hate speech, slurs, or offensive language.
  • The player who creates a guild becomes the guildmaster and accepts full responsibility for moderation within their guild.

3. GUILDMASTER RESPONSIBILITIES

As guildmaster you agree to:

  • Enforce the General TOS and Guild TOS within your community
  • Not abuse moderation tools to harass or repeatedly target members without cause
  • Review and respond to suspension appeals in a timely manner
  • Not use your guild to coordinate cheating, harassment, or any activity that violates the General TOS

4. SUSPENSIONS AND REMOVALS

Guildmasters may suspend members for 24 hours, 7 days, 30 days, or permanently. Suspended members retain their Portal account — suspension affects guild access only.

Members may appeal a suspension through the guild page. Appeals are reviewed by the guildmaster. If you believe a guildmaster is abusing moderation powers, contact Portal administrators at kenetics.art@gmail.com.

The guildmaster cannot be suspended or removed by other members. Only Portal administrators may take action against a guildmaster.

5. GUILD CHAT

Guild chat is available only to active, non-suspended members. Messages are limited to 500 characters. The same conduct rules from the General TOS apply in guild chat.

Guild chat messages may be reviewed by Portal administrators for moderation purposes.

6. LEAVING AND DISBANDING

Any member may leave a guild at any time. To disband a guild, the guildmaster must remove all members and then leave. Abandoned guilds with no activity for 90 days may be automatically removed.

7. VIOLATION OF GUILD TOS

Guilds that are used to organize harassment, cheating, or other violations of the General TOS will be disbanded by Portal administrators and the guildmaster may face account suspension.
