KEN'S ARCADE
PORTAL GENERAL TOS
ADMIN CHARTER
Last updated: June 2025 · For Portal administrators only · Applies in addition to all other TOS

By accepting an admin role on kensgames.com you agree to the responsibilities and restrictions in this Charter. Violation of this Charter may result in immediate removal of admin privileges and account suspension.

1. ADMIN LEVELS

The Portal uses a 4-tier admin system:

  • Level 0 — Regular User: No admin privileges
  • Level 1 — Moderator: Can warn users and review reports
  • Level 2 — Admin: Can suspend users, manage tournaments, review guilds
  • Level 3 — Super Admin: Can promote/demote admins, manage all content
  • Superuser (kbingh): Full authority — cannot be demoted or removed by any admin

2. ADMIN RESPONSIBILITIES

As a Portal administrator you agree to:

  • Enforce the General TOS and Guild TOS fairly and consistently
  • Not use admin tools to target users based on personal disputes
  • Document your moderation decisions in the admin console
  • Treat all users with impartiality regardless of guild membership, personal relationships, or competitive standing
  • Keep admin credentials and Portal access secure

3. PROHIBITED ADMIN ACTIONS

Administrators are expressly prohibited from:

  • Attempting to demote, suspend, or remove the superuser account (kbingh)
  • Using admin powers to give unfair advantages in games or leaderboards
  • Accessing or exposing other users' private information
  • Sharing admin credentials or granting unauthorized access
  • Retaliating against users who file complaints about admin conduct

4. SUPERUSER AUTHORITY

The superuser account (kbingh / kenetics.art@gmail.com) has final authority over all Portal decisions. The superuser:

  • Cannot be removed, suspended, or demoted by any admin action
  • Has override authority on all moderation decisions
  • Is the final arbiter of appeals escalated beyond guild or admin level

5. ACCOUNTABILITY

All admin actions are logged with timestamps and actor identity. Logs are reviewed periodically. Admins found to be abusing their role will have privileges revoked immediately.

6. ACCEPTING THIS CHARTER

Admin privileges will not be granted until this Charter has been reviewed and accepted. Existing administrators who have not yet accepted this Charter will be prompted to do so on their next admin console login.
