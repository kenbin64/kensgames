📖 Rules at a Glance

  • Goal: move all 5 pegs into your Safe Zone.
  • Track: outer loop is steady; inner Fast Track is the shortcut.
  • Bullseye: safe position and a flexible exit with face cards.
  • Captures: landing on an opponent sends them home (Safe Zones are protected).

♟️ Strategy Corner

  • Tempo beats greed: take safe progress over risky leaps when ahead.
  • Threats matter: keep a peg “in range” to discourage passes.
  • Use the Fast Track to convert a good roll into a real swing.
  • Protect your entry: timing Safe Zone entry is everything.

Welcome to Fast Track!

🎲

Fast Track is a strategic racing board game for 2-6 players. Race your pegs around the board and into your safe zone to win!

The Board

🔵🔴🟢🟡🟣🟠

Each player has a color with 5 pegs. 4 start in the Holding Area, and 1 starts on the Diamond (Home) hole. Get all 5 pegs to your Safe Zone to win!

Moving Pegs

🎯

Roll the dice and move your pegs clockwise around the outer track. Land exactly on the Safe Zone Entry (2 spaces before your Safe Zone) to enter.

The Fast Track

🚀

The inner Fast Track is a shortcut! Enter through any colored pentagon (except your own) and exit at your own color. Risky but rewarding!

The Bullseye

⭕

Land on the center Bullseye for safety. Rolling J, Q, or K lets you exit to any Fast Track pentagon. Strategic positioning!

Sending Opponents Home

💥

Land on an opponent's peg to send them back to their Holding Area! But beware - they can do the same to you. Pegs in Safe Zones cannot be attacked.

Prestige Points

🏆

Earn prestige by winning games, making strategic plays, and sending opponents home. Rise through the ranks: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Diamond → Platinum!

♟️ Strategy

Openings

  • Advance two pegs early: it creates more legal moves later.
  • Don’t sprint a single peg unless it’s protected or decisive.
  • Use the Bullseye when you need stability more than speed.

Midgame

  • Watch “capture lanes”: leave opponents awkward choices.
  • Shortcut with intent: Fast Track should change who’s leading.
  • Protect your Safe Zone entry timing; avoid getting bounced.

Endgame

  • Prioritize guaranteed scoring over flashy collisions.
  • When ahead, reduce variance: safer paths, fewer gambles.
  • When behind, increase variance: aggressive Fast Track plays.

Want to learn hands-on? Start an Offline vs Bots match and practice entering Safe Zones under pressure.

📜 Fast Track History

Fast Track belongs to the family of classic race board games where the “track” is only half the story. The interesting part is the interaction: shortcuts, safety, and sending opponents home.

Why it stays fun

  • The Fast Track creates comebacks without feeling random.
  • Safe Zones reward timing and planning.
  • Collisions create tension and social moments.
